Satyam Shivam Sundaram

  • Humanity
  • Simplicity
  • Protector
  • The Destroyer

Shiva's role as the destroyer is not merely about ending, but about transformation and renewal. Tandava, represents the rhythmic cycle of creation, preservation, and destruction Shiva is also known as "Bholenath," the innocent one, who is easily pleased by devotion. who imparts wisdom and guidance to his devotees. Shiva's simplicity teaches the beauty of inner richness.
